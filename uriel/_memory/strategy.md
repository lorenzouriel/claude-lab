# Strategy

> Current focus and priorities. Updated when focus shifts.

## 90-day goal

Double followers across all channels and reach ~$20k/month from closed deals (services + products).

## KPIs we track

- Revenue (monthly)
- Leads generated
- Active clients
- Follower count (Substack, LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube)

## Main blocker

Content creation and organization — too much manual work in producing and structuring posts. Goal: only approve, not produce from scratch.

## Top automation target

Content creation and structuring — the most repetitive, draining weekly task. Everything from writing posts to organizing the content calendar should be handled by skills/agents; Uriel only reviews and approves.

## Priorities this quarter

1. **Content machine**: Automate the 3x weekly Substack pipeline so content gets drafted, structured, and queued — just needs final approval
2. **Deal flow**: Actively reach companies to sell AI agent setups and data engineering services; target $20k/month
3. **Product launch**: Package existing knowledge into a fundamentals course or e-book as first digital product revenue stream
