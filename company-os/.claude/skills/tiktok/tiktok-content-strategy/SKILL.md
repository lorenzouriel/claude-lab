# TikTok Content Strategy

Create TikTok content that drives app downloads and user engagement. Platform-specific patterns for virality, algorithm optimization, and conversion-focused content.

**Core principle:** TikTok success = Hook (3s) + Value + Call-to-action, optimized for the For You Page algorithm.

## When to Use

- Launching a new app or feature
- Planning a TikTok marketing strategy
- Creating viral content ideas
- Optimizing an existing TikTok presence
- Targeting Gen Z / young millennials
- Need for rapid user acquisition

---

## The TikTok Success Formula

### 1. The 3-Second Hook

First 3 seconds determine everything.

**Hook patterns that work:**
- **Problem/pain point:** "Phone anxiety is ruining your life..."
- **Shocking stat:** "97% of people don't know this..."
- **Pattern interrupt:** "Stop using [common thing]..."
- **Quick win:** "Here's how to [desired outcome] in 60 seconds"
- **Relatable moment:** "POV: You're trying to [relatable struggle]"

**Hook mistakes:**
- ❌ Slow intro ("Hey guys, today I'm going to...")
- ❌ Logos/branding first (people scroll)
- ❌ Text-heavy slides
- ❌ Asking people to wait ("Wait for it...")

---

### 2. Optimal Video Structure

**Winning pattern (15–30 seconds):**

| Time | Section | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 0–3s | Hook | Grab attention |
| 3–10s | Problem | Relate to audience pain |
| 10–20s | Solution | Your app solves this |
| 20–25s | Social proof | Quick testimonial/result |
| 25–30s | CTA | Download link in bio |

**Video length guidance:**
- **15–30s:** Highest completion rate, best for ads
- **30–60s:** Educational content, tutorials
- **60–90s:** Storytelling, deeper dives (only when retention is strong)

---

### 3. Trending Sounds Strategy

**When to use trending sounds:**
- Sound matches your content theme
- Sound adds value (not forced)
- Early in the trend curve (days 1–3)

**Where to find them:**
- TikTok Creative Center (ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter)
- For You page (note what's trending)
- Competitor analysis

**Sound selection:**
- Choose sounds with <50k uses (early trend)
- Match energy level to your content
- Avoid overused sounds (>1M uses)

---

### 4. Hashtag Strategy

**Hashtag mix (3–5 total):**

```
1 × Niche hashtag     (10k–100k posts)   e.g. #phoneappanxiety
1 × Medium hashtag    (100k–1M posts)    e.g. #mentalhealth
1 × Broad hashtag     (1M+ posts)        e.g. #fyp
1 × App name hashtag                     e.g. #YourAppName
1 × CTA hashtag                          e.g. #linkinbio
```

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Only broad hashtags (#fyp alone)
- ❌ More than 7 hashtags
- ❌ Irrelevant trending tags

| Evening wind-down | 7–9 PM |

Test your own audience: post at different times, track performance, double down on best performers.

## Content Pillars

| Pillar | Share | Format |
|---|---|---|
| Problem/Solution | 50% | Show problem → demo app → before/after |
| Social Proof | 20% | User testimonials, results, download milestones |
| Education | 20% | Tips, "did you know" facts, how-to |
| Trending/Entertainment | 10% | Trend participation, humor, relatable memes |

**Examples:**

- **Problem/Solution:** "POV: You've been staring at your phone for 10 minutes trying to make one call. Here's what finally helped me... [show app]"
- **Social Proof:** "This app helped me go from avoiding calls to making 10+ calls per day. Here's how... [download CTA]"
- **Education:** "3 signs you have phone anxiety (and what to do about it) [app mention]"
- **Entertainment:** "When the phone rings and you immediately panic... (relatable trend format) [subtle app mention]"

## Viral Content Patterns

**Pattern 1 — The Transformation**
"Day 1 vs Day 30 using [your app]" — clear before/after, concrete metrics, authentic testimonial.

**Pattern 2 — The Secret Hack**
"The [app name] feature nobody uses but should" — reveals hidden value, quick tutorial, drives downloads from curiosity.

**Pattern 3 — The Relatable Struggle**
"Things only [target audience] understand" — list format, highly relatable pain points, app as solution (subtle).

**Pattern 4 — The Tutorial**
"How to [solve problem] in under 60 seconds" — fast-paced, clear value, app as tool.

**Pattern 5 — The Challenge**
"Try the [challenge] for 7 days" — invites participation, use app to track, encourages UGC.

## Algorithm Optimization

TikTok ranks content by:

1. **Completion rate** — keep videos short (15–30s), strong hook, fast pacing, no dead time
2. **Engagement** — ask questions in captions, prompt comments with "What do you think?"
3. **Rewatches** — hide value at end, dense information, satisfying loops
4. **Shares** — highly relatable content, useful tools, emotional resonance
5. **Watch time** — 90% completion of a short video beats 50% of a long one; optimize for retention rate

## Call-to-Action Patterns

**In video:**
- "Link in bio to download"
- "Search [App Name] in App Store"
- "Comment 'APP' for the link"

**In caption:**
- "Get the app: [link]"
- "Download free: link in bio"
- "#linkinbio"

**Don't:**
- ❌ Long URLs in caption
- ❌ "Click link in bio" without a value proposition
- ❌ Pushy sales language

## Content Calendar

**Posting frequency:**
- Minimum: 3–5 videos/week
- Optimal: 1–2 videos/day
- Maximum: 3 videos/day (avoid spam perception)

**Weekly mix:**

| Day | Content type |
|---|---|
| Monday | Problem/solution |
| Tuesday | Educational tip |
| Wednesday | User testimonial / social proof |
| Thursday | Trending participation |
| Friday | Tutorial / how-to |
| Weekend | Relatable / entertainment |

## Creator Collaboration

**Micro-influencers (10k–100k followers):**
- More authentic engagement
- Lower cost ($100–500 per video)
- Niche audience match

**Collaboration types:**
- Sponsored posts (pay creator)
- Affiliate links (commission per download)
- Product gifting (free access)

**Selection criteria:**
- Audience matches your target demographic
- Engagement rate >5%
- Content style matches your brand
- Previous sponsored content quality

## Analytics

**Track these metrics:**

| Metric | Benchmark |
|---|---|
| Completion rate | >50% |
| Engagement rate | >5% |
| Profile visit rate | >2% |
| Download conversion | >10% of link clicks |

Also track: views, profile visits, link clicks, and app downloads from TikTok.

## Quick Win Tactics

1. **Stitch/Duet with trending creators** — find viral videos in your niche, add value with your perspective, tag original creator.
2. **TikTok SEO** — keywords in captions, keywords in on-screen text, searchable hashtags.
3. **Series format** — "Day X of using [app]" or "Tip #X for [problem]" builds follow-for-more.
4. **Behind-the-scenes** — show app development, feature sneak peeks, founder story.
