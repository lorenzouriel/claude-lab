# Platform Rules Reference (2026)

Per-platform algorithm rules, cadence, timing, and non-negotiables. Loaded by the social-content-planner when building the weekly calendar.

---

## LinkedIn

### Algorithm signals (ranked)
1. Comments — highest weight, especially early comments in first 2 hours
2. Reactions (PRAISE, EMPATHY, INTEREST) — weighted above LIKE
3. Shares — distribution signal
4. Saves — secondary signal
5. LIKE — weakest signal

### Cadence
- **3-5 posts/week** — optimal. 6+ triggers cannibalization; under 3 deprioritizes the account.
- **10-20 comments/day** on other creators — comments drive more inbound than posts.
- **Monday = commenting day** (no original post). Best day to engage on others.
- **Tue/Wed/Thu** — top days for B2B reach. Avoid Fri after 2 PM, Sat/Sun.

### Posting times (B2B)
- Tue-Thu: 7:30-9:00 AM local (strongest)
- Mon/Fri: 8:00-9:30 AM (secondary)

### Format rules
- **No external links in post body** — move to first comment. Algo penalty is severe.
- **0-2 hashtags** — at the end of the post.
- **900-1300 chars** sweet spot for text posts.
- **Hook in first 210 chars** (before "... see more").
- **Double line-breaks** between ideas — single breaks are invisible on mobile.
- **Carousels:** 5-10 slides; save rate is the primary signal.

### Non-negotiables
- No em dashes
- No "In today's fast-paced world" openers
- No generic CTAs ("What do you think?", "Tag someone who needs this")
- Product pillar: max 1 post/week

## X (Twitter)

### Algorithm signals (ranked)
1. Replies — highest signal. A tweet with 20 replies beats 200 likes.
2. Quote tweets with commentary — strong distribution + engagement signal
3. Bookmarks — saves signal
4. Retweets — distribution signal, lower weight than replies
5. Likes — weakest signal

### Cadence
- **1-3 original posts/day** — 8+ triggers spam signal
- **10-15 substantive replies/day** to other accounts — the most under-used growth lever
- **Sunday = reply day only** — no original posts; clear the week's comment threads
- **Threads: max 1/week** initially — readers need to choose to click "show thread"

### Posting times
- 8-10 AM local (morning commute)
- 12-1 PM local (lunch)
- 7-9 PM local (evening)

### Format rules
- **No external links in the tweet body** — move to first reply. ~50% reach penalty.
- **0-1 hashtags** — more signals spam.
- **First 5-8 words are the hook** — visible before truncation in feed.
- **Threads:** tweet 1 must stand alone. 5-8 tweets optimal.
- **Quote tweets: 2-3/week** — you get the original's audience + your own.

### Non-negotiables
- No "follow me for more content like this"
- No "RT if you agree"
- No all-caps anywhere
- No hedging in opinion tweets ("might", "perhaps", "sort of")

## Instagram

### Algorithm signals (ranked)

**Feed posts:**
1. Saves — strongest signal. Write content worth bookmarking.
2. Shares to Stories — distribution signal
3. Comments (especially depth + length)
4. Likes — weakest

**Reels:**
1. Shares to Stories — #1 reach signal
2. Completion rate — 80%+ = strong push
3. Replays
4. Saves (secondary for educational Reels)
5. Comments + replies within 2 hours

**Carousels:**
1. Swipe-through rate
2. Saves
3. Shares (screenshot-worthy slides)

### Cadence
- **4-5 feed posts/week** — optimal. Under 3 = deprioritized.
- **Daily Stories** — separate from feed; doesn't compete with reach. Critical for existing audience warmth.
- **Reels: 2-3/week** — quality over volume. Completion rate matters.
- **Carousels: 1-2/week** — highest save format.

### Posting times
- B2B: Tue-Thu 8-10 AM local
- Consumer: Mon-Fri 11 AM-1 PM, 7-9 PM local
- Weekend: Sat 10 AM-12 PM for lifestyle content

### Format rules
- **No clickable links in captions** — Instagram doesn't make them clickable. Use "link in bio" with context.
- **0-5 hashtags** — 3-5 targeted > 30 generic. Instagram has deprioritized hashtag discovery.
- **First 2 lines = hook** (~90 chars before "more" fold).
- **Reel hook in first 3 seconds** — visual action + on-screen text + spoken line all in sec 0-3.
- **Reel length:** 7-30s (entertainment), 30-60s (educational). Above 90s = reach penalty.

### Non-negotiables
- No "Double tap if you agree!"
- No "Save this post!" without a reason
- No describing what's already visible in the image
- Reply to all comments within 2 hours of posting (the boost window)

## TikTok

### Algorithm signals (ranked)
1. Completion rate — #1 signal. 80%+ = strong FYP push.
2. Shares — strongest distribution signal
3. Replays — very high satisfaction signal
4. Comments — especially creator replies within 2 hours
5. Likes — weakest
6. Follows from the video — indicates conversion

### The FYP mechanism
- TikTok recommends based on **content signals, not account size**
- New accounts can go viral on video 1 if completion rate is high
- Testing cascade: seed (~500) → expanded (~10k) → broad (~100k+) → viral

### Cadence
- **1-2 original videos/day** for growth — consistency > volume
- **2-3 duets or stitches/week** — boosted by algo, low production cost
- **1-2 trending sound videos/week** — ~20-40% initial reach boost
- **Never delete videos** — old TikToks can go viral months later

### Posting times
- 6-8 AM local (early risers)
- 12-2 PM local (lunch)
- 7-9 PM local (peak — highest volume)

### Format rules
- **First 3 seconds = the entire game** — 80% of viewers decide here
- **On-screen text visible in sec 1** — serves muted viewers
- **Spoken hook in sec 0-3** — must work without the screen
- **No intro before the hook** — cut "Hey guys", "So today", "Welcome back" entirely
- **Duets/Stitches:** must add genuine value; no "great point!" stitches

### Non-negotiables
- Never start with "Hey guys", "So today I want to talk about", "Welcome back"
- Never use "Don't forget to like and subscribe" (YouTube phrasing — kills credibility)
- Never delete a video (kills long-tail virality potential)
- Reply to ALL comments within 2 hours of posting (15-25% reach lift)

## Cross-platform comparison

| Rule | LinkedIn | X | Instagram | TikTok |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Links in post | First comment | First reply | Bio only | Bio only |
| Hashtags | 0-2 | 0-1 | 0-5 | 0-3 |
| Top signal | Comments | Replies | Saves / Shares | Completion rate |
| Engagement window | 2 hours | 1 hour | 2 hours | 2 hours |
| Posting days | Tue-Thu | Every day | Every day | Every day |
| Content cadence | 3-5/week | 7-14/week | 4-5/week | 7-12/week |
| New account reach | Medium | Medium | Low | Very high |
