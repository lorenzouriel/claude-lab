# Unified Pillar Framework

The same 3 pillars apply across all platforms. What changes is the format, length, tone, and entry point — not the underlying content strategy.

---

## Pillar 1 — Insight / Authority (40%)

**What it is:** Content that proves you know something worth knowing. Data, analyses, processes, strong opinions backed by evidence.

**Why it's 40% everywhere:** This is the pillar that builds followers and saves. People follow and bookmark accounts that teach them something. Without it, growth stalls.

### Platform expression

| Platform | Best formats | Hook types | Length target |
|---|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | Long-form text, carousel | F7 Odd-Precision Money, F10 Contrarian Historical, F8 Paid-vs-Free | 900-1300 chars |
| X | Thread, number tweet, data tweet | T2 Number Shock, T9 Thread Bait, T4 Hot Take | Thread: 5-8 tweets |
| Instagram | Carousel, educational Reel | C2 Bold Claim, C3 Number, C6 Reveal | Carousel: 6-10 slides |
| TikTok | Tutorial script, listicle script | H3 Listicle Promise, H5 Research Authority, H6 Reveal | 30-60 sec |

**Signs it's working:** High saves (LinkedIn/Instagram), bookmarks (X), comments asking follow-up questions (all platforms).

**Signs it's failing:** Generic advice that everyone already knows. The content is too abstract — no specific numbers, no named entities, no firsthand receipts.

---

## Pillar 2 — Personal / Narrative (30%)

**What it is:** Real experiences, failures, confessions, behind-the-scenes. Content where the creator — not just the information — is the value.

**Why it's 30% everywhere:** Insight content gets you found. Personal content makes people stay. Trust is built through vulnerability and specificity, not polish.

### Platform expression

| Platform | Best formats | Hook types | Tone target |
|---|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | Confession post, year-pivot, time-anchor | F4 Time-Anchor Confession, F3 Year-over-Year Pivot | Professional vulnerability |
| X | Confession tweet, story thread | T3 Confession, T10 How-I | Raw, direct |
| Instagram | Single image + caption, story Reel | C4 Confession, C7 Story Opener | Warm, specific |
| TikTok | Story script, behind-the-scenes | H1 Curiosity / Did-Something, H1 Story | Unpolished, spoken-word |

**Signs it's working:** High comments, DMs from people who relate, saves from followers (not new people).

**Signs it's failing:** Vague confessions ("I used to struggle"). Stakes are absent. The vulnerability is performed, not real — no specific numbers, dates, or named decisions.

---

## Pillar 3 — Reach / Conversation (30%)

**What it is:** Content designed to reach new people and create conversation. Replies, quote tweets, duets, polls, relatable posts, trending formats.

**Why it's 30% everywhere:** Insight and Personal content converts people who already found you. This pillar finds new people and makes the algorithm distribute your content further.

### Platform expression

| Platform | Best formats | What drives reach |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | Poll, community spotlight, comment-gate | Comments (LinkedIn weights them heavily), viral poll reach |
| X | Reply to big accounts, QT with add, question tweet | Replies > RTs > likes; QTs expose you to the original audience |
| Instagram | Short Reel (7-30s), before/after | Shares to Stories (top signal), completion rate |
| TikTok | Duet, stitch, trending sound video | Completion rate, FYP for-you recommendation |

**Signs it's working:** Reach to non-followers, new follows from this content, engagement from accounts that don't normally see you.

**Signs it's failing:** High likes from existing followers but no new accounts. Content is reaching the same 500 people every week.

---

## Pillar ratio adjustments by goal

The 40/30/30 default works for knowledge-based creators. Adjust based on the week's goal:

| Goal | Insight | Personal | Reach |
|---|---|---|---|
| Grow audience fast | 30% | 20% | 50% |
| Build trust / convert | 35% | 45% | 20% |
| Establish thought leadership | 55% | 25% | 20% |
| Inbound leads | 40% | 30% | 30% (target prospects) |
| Launch something | 35% | 30% | 35% (+ product pillar 10-15% on LinkedIn) |

**Hard rule across all platforms:** No pillar below 15%. Dropping further makes the account one-dimensional — audiences feel it before they can articulate it.

---

## Cross-platform pillar consistency

When running multiple platforms, the same weekly pillar assignment applies to all. If this week is a "Personal / Narrative" heavy week (post-launch, post-failure, reflection), that tone should be visible across all platforms — not just LinkedIn.

**Example:**
- LinkedIn: F4 Time-Anchor Confession (full vulnerability, long-form)
- X: T3 Confession tweet (the sharpest single line from the LinkedIn post)
- Instagram: C4 Confession caption + single image Reel
- TikTok: Story script with H1 hook (the moment of maximum tension)

Same week's energy. Four different entries into the same idea.
