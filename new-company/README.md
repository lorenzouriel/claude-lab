# CompanyOS

> Your business runs on this folder.

CompanyOS is an AI operating system for your company built on top of Claude Code. It knows who you are, how you speak, and what you are working on — and it gets smarter every session.

You set it up once. After that, you just work.

---

## What it replaces

**A content team.**
CompanyOS plans your week across LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, and X from a single theme. It writes platform-native posts, creates branded carousels at 1080×1350, scripts Reels, humanizes drafts, and schedules or publishes directly. No agency needed for recurring content.

**A paid media specialist.**
It builds complete Google Ads campaigns — keywords grouped by intent, 15 headlines per ad group, all 6 CSV files ready to import into Google Ads Editor — and reads your weekly Meta + Google exports to surface what to scale, what to pause, and where budget is burning.

**A scattered stack of tools.**
SEO research, competitor analysis, Google Business Profile, data analysis, professional emails, PDF/Word/PowerPoint/Excel documents, cold email sequences, proposal drafts — all in one place, with your business context already loaded.

---

## Who it is for

**Solopreneur / creator**
You produce and publish. CompanyOS handles the content pipeline — from topic ideation to branded carousel to scheduled post — so you stay in flow instead of in Canva.

**Freelancer**
You deliver for clients. CompanyOS organizes by project, drafts proposals, generates deliverables in the right format (PDF, DOCX, PPTX), and handles the emails and follow-ups that eat your afternoons.

**Agency**
You manage multiple clients at once. CompanyOS gives each client their own project folder, runs platform-specific content for each account, generates ads reports, and keeps the production pipeline moving without bottlenecks.

**Company**
You have departments and processes. CompanyOS connects marketing, commercial, operations, and finance into a single workspace — content, campaigns, reports, and internal documents in one place that everyone can reference.

## Get started in 3 steps

**Step 1 — Install Claude Code**
Download it at [claude.ai/download](https://claude.ai/download). It is free to start.

**Step 2 — Get CompanyOS**
```
git clone https://github.com/mazzeoia/CompanyOS.git
cd CompanyOS
claude .
```
Or open Claude Code and say: *"Clone https://github.com/mazzeoia/CompanyOS.git and run /install"*

**Step 3 — Run setup**
In Claude Code, type:
```
/system install
```
It will interview you about your business — one question at a time, 5 minutes. At the end, the system knows who you are, how you write, and what you are working on. It will not ask again.

## How it works

**`memory/`** is the working memory — who the company is, how it speaks, what is in focus this week. Claude reads this silently before every answer. The better the memory, the better the system.

**`brain/`** is the second brain, organized with the PARA method. Inbox for raw drops, projects for active work with deadlines, areas for ongoing responsibilities, resources for reusable reference (your brand identity lives here), archive for what is done.

**`.claude/skills/`** are the skills — 70+ capabilities organized into categories. System utilities, content creation, platform publishing (Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, X, YouTube, newsletters), document formats, research, and business operations. Each skill knows your business context before it runs.

**`output/`** is where everything lands — content, SEO files, campaigns, documents. Versioned on GitHub. Everything stays yours.

AI is not a tool your company uses. It is the operating system it runs on.
