# 2-areas/ — ongoing responsibilities

Standards you maintain over time with **no end date**: marketing, SEO,
sales, finance, operations, hiring. Unlike projects, an area is never
"done" — you keep a level of quality in it.

Use areas for durable knowledge a responsibility accumulates: your SEO
playbook, the sales pipeline notes, recurring-content guidelines.

```
2-areas/
├── marketing/
├── seo/
├── sales/
└── finance/
```

Generated deliverables don't live here — those go to `output/`. Areas hold
the thinking; `output/` holds the artifacts.
