# Identity - Solo Creator

> Visual identity used with a real CompanyOS client - solo creator,
> personal brand, audience-first. Adapt yours in `identity/design-guide.md`.

---

## Colors

- **Main background:** #FAF7F2
- **Highlight / CTA color:** #C96442
- **Main text:** #1C1917
- **Alternative background / cards:** #F3EFE8
- **Forbidden color:** electric blue, very saturated colors

---

## Typography

- **Titles and highlights:** Instrument Serif (italic, weight 400)
- **Body, subtitles, and buttons:** Bricolage Grotesque (weight 400-700)
- **Title weight:** regular italic (lightness is part of the style)

---

## General style

Warm, human, and editorial. Earth tones with an off-white background. It
feels like a beautiful book or a personal brand journal. Terracotta
(#C96442) appears in CTAs, pills, and text highlights.

---

## Key elements

- Borders: dashed, rounded (border-radius: 20px)
- Card border radius: 20px
- Buttons: pill with border-radius: 50px, terracotta background
- Shadows: subtle (0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.06))

---

## What to NEVER do

- Pure white background (#FFFFFF) - always use off-white (#FAF7F2)
- Straight borders (solid without radius) - feels stiff and generic
- Lemon yellow as an accent - clashes with the warm tone

---
