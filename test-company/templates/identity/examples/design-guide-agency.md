# Identity - Performance Agency

> Visual identity used with a real CompanyOS client - performance
> agency, direct tone, no decoration. Adapt yours in `identity/design-guide.md`.

---

## Colors

- **Main background:** #0F0F0F
- **Highlight / CTA color:** #FF5C35
- **Main text:** #F5F5F5
- **Alternative background / cards:** #1A1A1A
- **Forbidden color:** lime green, neon colors in general

---

## Typography

- **Titles and highlights:** Syne (bold, weight 700-800)
- **Body, subtitles, and buttons:** DM Sans (weight 400-500)
- **Title weight:** extrabold

---

## General style

Dark and bold. Performance agency interface: no fluff, no decoration.
Every element has a function. Orange (#FF5C35) appears only in CTAs and
critical highlights.

---

## Key elements

- Borders: solid 1px with low opacity
- Card border radius: 12px
- Buttons: pill with border-radius: 50px, orange background
- Shadows: no

---

## What to NEVER do

- Use green in any tone
- Cards with too much rounding (> 16px feels childish for our positioning)
- Gradient as a full-section background

---

## Logo

- **File:** identity/logo.svg
- **Version for light background:** identity/logo-dark.svg
- **Where to use:** final carousel slide (CTA), proposal header, presentation slides
- **Suggested size:** width between 120-200px in HTML files
